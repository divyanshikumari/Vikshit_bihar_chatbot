📌 About This Chatbot

This chatbot is based on the official data of DSTTE Bihar technical colleges.

It provides information about Engineering and Polytechnic colleges across all districts of Bihar.

📂 What Information Is Available?

  • 🎓 Branch-wise registered student count
  • 🏠 District-wise hostel and bed availability
  • 👨‍🏫 College-wise professor statistics
  • 💼 Year-wise placement reports
  • 📋 District-wise admission reports
